WELCOME TO DEREK'S PROFILE

PROJECT IDEA

M y project is something through research I discovered has already been done but not in the full
capacity of which I want to do it.
I am thinking of a mobile application of a compendium of board games from all over the world with
a catch.
The original application will be free but only have a limited number of common games (maybe 3)
but with the ability to purch​ase more games through microtransactions. $1.99 each etc.
I chose this route because my primary aim for this course is to develop skills to make money.
Therefore I will be putting in micro transactions. It’s all about profit for me.
There are gaming compendium apps however none of them have microtransactions to download
more games and that I believe is something missing in this genre.
This project will involved extensive research into the rules and regulations of all known and
unknown games.
Detailed artwork down to the letter of the boards, pieces, mascots etc.
It will also involve licencing fees and negotiations from the boardgame manufacturers in order to be
able to use their mascots and original names of the games.

Description:
A compendium of well known board games from all over the world with multiplay ability depending
on max amount of players ex: monopoly.
People will be able to download the gaming app which will initially come with around 3 well known
games and they will have the ability to purchase more games and also possibly collectible items and
level up bonuses depending on the game.
These games could include child friendly games like “Snakes and Ladders” as well as more mature
games like “Roulette, Poker and BlackJack”
There will be childproof settings which will prevent kids from accessing the more mature content as
well as payment security via paypal which will be behind a secure server and 2nd degree
authentication.

Tools: 
Tools to create this app will include but not limited to Adobe Photoshop and other imaging software,
I have done work on Adobe Photoshop before in creating 3d images ad well as animations however I
would most likely employ a digital graphics engineer as my knowledge is very limited.
Programming languages such as Kotlin, C++, C#, python to name a few will be used in order to be
able to complete more complex graphics and algorithms.
Reference: harikian78 @harkiran78
https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/top-programming-languages-for-android-app-development/

Outcome:
If this project was able to be developed eventually I believe it will have a positive reception
worldwide due to the demand for online boardgames.
Everything is going digital now and the ability to play any games you want in one application will
reduce the need for multiple apps on the one phone.
There will be people who will be happy to stick with the free version of the app and deal with the
advertisements as well however I believe a large percentage would download 1 or 2 extra games.
