WELCOME TO DEREK'S PROFILE

PERSONAL INFORMATION



Hi I am Derek, I was born in Auburn hospital on July 12, 1980. I am of Aboriginal decent but also
descended from the First Fleet.
I am an avid gamer, preferring games of the Sandbox RPG and​ MMORPG genres, However will
generally play anything including FPS and sports games.
My first MMORPG was Everquest which I believe set the bar for future MMORPG and was eventually
surpassed by WoW as the new bar.
I am married to a filipina whom I met locally and have 3 boys Aged 10, 9 and 3 months including a
stepson.
My interest in IT started at a very young age when my family bought their first computer in the late
80’s early 90’s which was an Amiga 500.
I then started building my own personal computers in my teens and hosted my own BBS back when
we still used 2400 baud rate modems.
I decided to study IT after a push from my wife due to being off work for an extended time thanks to
work injury.
My plan is to go all the way through to Bachelor of IT possibly even Masters and start my own
mobile apps development business.
